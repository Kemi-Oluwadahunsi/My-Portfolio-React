# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Hiring managers at mid-to-large companies evaluating for senior frontend/MFE architect roles, and potential freelance/consulting clients assessing technical depth and delivery capability. They arrive from LinkedIn, GitHub, or a direct link, scan quickly, and decide within seconds whether to invest further attention.

## Product Purpose

A personal engineering portfolio for Kemi Oluwadahunsi — senior software engineer specializing in enterprise React, micro-frontend architecture (Webpack 5 Module Federation), IAM/security engineering, and technical writing. The site demonstrates architectural thinking, production delivery, and engineering depth through real case studies rather than decorative filler. Success: a visitor leaves convinced this is a senior-caliber engineer they want to hire or collaborate with.

## Brand Personality

**Elegant, polished, authoritative.** The voice is precise without being cold, confident without being loud. Every element earns its place. The portfolio reads like a senior engineer's work: deliberate, refined, technically grounded. Premium presence — not flashy, not minimal for the sake of it, but the kind of restraint that signals expertise.

## Anti-references

- Generic template portfolios (cookie-cutter layouts, identical card grids, stock hero patterns)
- Overly playful or childish sites (bouncy animations, emoji-heavy, cartoon illustrations)
- Too corporate or stiff (enterprise brochure feel, stock photography, committee-designed blandness)
- "AI slop" aesthetics (gradient text, glassmorphism everywhere, hero-metric dashboards, neon-on-dark crypto vibes)

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't decorate.** Every visual element should communicate engineering competence. If it doesn't prove capability or aid comprehension, remove it.
2. **Senior presence.** The site should feel like the work of someone who has shipped at scale. Restraint, intentionality, and polish signal seniority more than complexity.
3. **Depth on demand.** Surface layer is scannable in 10 seconds. Each layer deeper reveals more technical substance — case studies, architecture diagrams, implementation details.
4. **Earned confidence.** Let the work speak. Bold where the results justify boldness, quiet everywhere else.
5. **Craft as proof.** The portfolio itself is a deliverable. Its performance, accessibility, and attention to detail are evidence of engineering standards.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. Reduced motion support for vestibular sensitivities. Sufficient color contrast ratios. Keyboard navigation throughout. Screen reader-friendly landmarks and alt text.
